    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do you sell or install the physical metal gates?

    expand_more

    No. 13AL specialises strictly in autogate motors — not gate fabrication. We supply and install high-quality automatic gate motors (swing arm and folding arm systems) onto your existing metal, aluminium, or wooden gates in Ipoh and surrounding Perak areas.

    If you need a new gate structure built, we recommend engaging a separate gate fabricator first — we can then fit our motor system to it at any time.

    What is the warranty for the autogate motor?

    expand_more

    All 13AL autogate motor installations come with a 1-Year Warranty on parts and workmanship — our Peace of Mind Guarantee. This covers motor defects, wiring faults, and operational failures under normal usage.

    The warranty does not cover damage caused by floods, lightning strikes, severe power surges, or external physical impact, as with all electronic equipment in Malaysia's tropical climate.

    How much does it cost to install an autogate motor in Ipoh?

    expand_more

    Autogate installation in Ipoh starts from RM1,XXX for a standard swing or folding arm gate system. This includes the motor unit, 3 remote controls, built-in backup battery, manual release key, concealed wiring, and 1-year warranty — with zero hidden fees.

    Final pricing depends on location, any extra services & complexity. Send us photos or a short video of your gate on WhatsApp for a free, exact quote — usually responded to within the hour.

    How long does it take to install an autogate motor?

    expand_more

    A standard autogate motor installation takes approximately half a day — around 4 to 5 hours. Our certified technicians install the motor, safely conceal all wiring, weatherproof the connections, program all remote controls, and rigorously test the gate and backup battery before handover.

    More complex installations (e.g. heavy wrought-iron gates or older hinges requiring reinforcement) may take slightly longer. We'll advise you during assessment.

    How do I open my automatic gate during a TNB power outage?

    expand_more

    All 13AL autogate systems include a built-in backup battery. The moment TNB power is cut, the gate automatically switches to battery power and continues operating normally — no manual pushing required, and no configuration needed on your part.

    The backup battery provides up to 24 hours of uninterrupted gate operation. A manual release key is also included in every package as a secondary emergency option.

    Do you provide autogate repair or maintenance services?

    expand_more

    Yes. 13AL provides professional autogate motor repair and servicing across Ipoh and Perak. Whether your autogate is from us or another brand, WhatsApp us a short video of the issue — we'll assess it quickly and provide a transparent repair quote.

    We can help with:

    • Loud or grinding noises — usually worn gears or dry bearings
    • Gate moving very slowly or getting stuck — often a torque or alignment issue
    • Gate failing to open or close completely — limit switch or motor fault
    • Remote control not working — frequency pairing, battery, or board issues
    • Gate not working after power outage — backup battery may need replacement

    What areas in Perak do you cover for autogate installation?

    expand_more

    13AL provides autogate motor installation and repair across a wide coverage area in Perak — from Kuala Kangsar in the north all the way down to Kampar in the south. Travel charges may apply for locations outside Ipoh centre.

    • North (Kuala Kangsar to Ipoh): Kuala Kangsar, Padang Rengas, Sungai Siput, Chemor, Kanthan, Klebang, Meru, Meru Raya, Jelapang, Tanjung Rambutan.
    • Ipoh Central & East: Ipoh Town, Greentown, Canning Garden, Taman Ipoh, Bercham, Tambun, Sunway City Ipoh, Tasek, Ampang, Gunung Rapat, Bandar Seri Botani, Taman Cempaka.
    • Ipoh West & South: Silibin, Menglembu, Falim, Buntong, Pasir Puteh, Station 18, Pengkalan, Lahat.
    • South (Batu Gajah to Kampar): Batu Gajah, Pusing, Tronoh, Bemban, Simpang Pulai, Gopeng, Lawan Kuda, Malim Nawar, Kampar.

    Not sure if we cover your area? Drop us a WhatsApp message with your location — we'll confirm within minutes.
